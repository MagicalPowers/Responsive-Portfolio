About Me

I was born in the early afternoon, though I do not recall it. I, Zachary Powers, am named of course after the late great actor Ricardo Montalban. I am a skilled carpenter and a trained engineer. I have been constructing, maintaining, troubleshooting, and upgrading complex systems and projects for over ten years. I recently lost my mother to esophageal cancer and I have a four-month old GS puppy.
